About Our System

The Road Safety System aims to improve road safety by allowing users to log accidents, view accident statistics, and quickly access emergency services.

Features

  • Add Accidents: Log details of accidents including location, time, and severity.
  • View Records: View and filter accident records based on various criteria.
  • Emergency Services: Quick access to emergency services and contact information.
  • Data Analysis: Generate reports and statistics to help identify accident-prone areas and trends.
